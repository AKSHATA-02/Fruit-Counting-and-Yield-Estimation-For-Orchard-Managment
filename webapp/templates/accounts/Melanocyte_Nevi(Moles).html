Disease Precaution For Melanocyte Nevi (Moles)

Important Precautions

  • Monitor changes: Use the ABCDE rule to check for signs of melanoma (Asymmetry, Border irregularity, Color variation, Diameter larger than 6 mm, Evolving shape).
  • Wear sunscreen: Protect moles from UV exposure with a high-SPF sunscreen.
  • Limit sun exposure: Avoid tanning and long exposure to the sun.
  • Perform regular skin exams: Check your body regularly for new moles or changes in existing ones. Visit your dermatologist for annual checks.
  • Consider mole removal: If a mole is located in an area prone to friction or if you notice changes, removal might be an option.
