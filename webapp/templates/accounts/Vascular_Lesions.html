Disease Precaution For Vascular Lesions

Important Precautions

  • Avoid injury:Vascular lesions, especially cherry angiomas, can be prone to bleeding if scratched or injured, so protect them from trauma.
  • Wear sunscreen: Protect the affected areas from UV radiation to prevent further damage.
  • Monitor changes: Watch for any increase in size or number of lesions.
  • Seek medical advice: If vascular lesions become painful, grow rapidly, or bleed frequently, consult a healthcare professional.
  • Consider cosmetic treatments: If lesions are bothersome, treatments like laser therapy or cryotherapy may be options to reduce their appearance.
