Disease Precaution For Actinic Keratosis

Important Precautions

  • Limit sun exposure: Avoid direct sunlight, especially between 10 AM and 4 PM, when UV radiation is strongest.
  • Wear sunscreen: Use broad-spectrum sunscreen with an SPF of 30 or higher on exposed skin every 2 hours, and after swimming or sweating.
  • Wear protective clothing: Consider hats, sunglasses, and long sleeves to reduce sun exposure.
  • Regular skin checks: Perform monthly self-examinations of your skin to monitor for any changes or new growths.
  • Visit a dermatologist regularly: Have your skin examined annually or as recommended by your healthcare provider.
