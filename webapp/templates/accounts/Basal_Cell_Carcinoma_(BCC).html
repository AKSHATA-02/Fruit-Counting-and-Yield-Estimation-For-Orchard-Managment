Disease Precaution For Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC)

Important Precautions

  • Avoid tanning beds: These can increase the risk of developing skin cancer.
  • Seek shade: Always stay in the shade when possible to reduce UV exposure.
  • Wear protective clothing: Hats, UV-blocking clothing, and sunglasses can help protect you from harmful UV rays.
  • Get regular screenings: Have a dermatologist check your skin regularly, especially if you have a history of skin cancer.
  • Use sunscreen: Apply sunscreen with an SPF of 50 or higher to prevent sunburn and reduce the risk of further damage.
